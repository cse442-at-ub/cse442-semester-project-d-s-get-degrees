CSL's CS:GO Season Suspended in the Midst of Play-offs

March 20th, 2020

Following the movement of many North American colleges to an online learning model, Colegiate Star League, or CSL, has decided to suspend its spring 2020 CS:GO season, despite being deep into play-offs. This past weekend, prior to the cancelation, competing teams saw themselves amongst the top 4 teams in the country playing college Counter Strike. While cancelation has not been confirmed, it looks like months shall pass before any matches can be played again.

What We Do

UB Esports is the hub for video games at the University at Buffalo. We strive to expand our community and grow as an organization. Our competitive scene involves many teams spanning different games, genres and leagues. We currently have teams for Overwatch, League of Legends, Counter Strike, and Rainbow Six Siege, with upcoming teams for Rocket League, Valorant, Call of Duty and more. While we are called UB Esports, we also have a strong casual community. We have a large communities for Animal Crossing and Minecraft, and are open to invite more games into our umbrella. We highly recommend joining our Discord Server to learn more about our community.
